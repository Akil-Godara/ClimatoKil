 
# 🌍 ClimatoKil - South Asia Climate Data Archive

A comprehensive climate data visualization platform tracking environmental changes across **Afghanistan, Pakistan, and India** from 1960 to present.

---

**Website:** 
[climatokil.pages.dev](https://climatokil.pages.dev/)

## 📖 About

ClimatoKil is a data-driven archive that visualizes long-term climate transformations in South Asia. The platform provides accessible, interactive visualizations of climate change impacts across three nations most vulnerable to environmental shifts.

### Key Features

- **10 Climate Indicators** per country (Temperature, Precipitation, Wind Pressure, Atmospheric Gases, Cryosphere, Soil, Water, El Niño/La Niña, Heatwaves, Wildfires)
- **Interactive Charts** showing trends from 1960-2024
- **Geographic Maps** with climate data overlays
- **Data Sources** from leading international agencies
- **Contact Form** for submitting climate research

---

## 🗺️ Countries Covered

### 🇦🇫 Afghanistan
- Temperature: +1.8°C since 1960
- Precipitation: -12% decrease
- Glacier loss: -25% in Hindu Kush
- Water resources: -30% reduction

### 🇵🇰 Pakistan
- Temperature: +1.5°C since 1960
- Precipitation: +8% (highly erratic)
- Glacier loss: -35% in Karakoram
- Water stress: Critical (1000m³ per capita)

### 🇮🇳 India
- Temperature: +1.3°C since 1960
- Precipitation: +10% (erratic monsoons)
- Glacier loss: -40% in Himalayas
- Water resources: -45% depletion

## 📊 Data Visualizations

### Interactive Charts
1. **Temperature Trends** - Line chart showing warming across all three countries
2. **Precipitation Patterns** - Bar chart of rainfall changes
3. **Glacier Mass Balance** - Line chart of ice loss over time
4. **Water Stress Index** - Water availability per capita trends

### Geographic Maps
- Interactive Leaflet map focused on South Asia
- Country boundaries with climate data overlays
- Major city markers with specific climate indicators
- Zoomable interface for detailed exploration

## 🏢 Data Sources

Climate data synthesized from reports by:

- **IPCC** - Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change
- **NOAA** - National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
- **NASA** - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
- **ISRO** - Indian Space Research Organisation
- **CNSA** - China National Space Administration
- **ESA** - European Space Agency
- **World Bank** - Climate Change Knowledge Portal
- **UNFCCC** - Greenhouse Gas Inventory

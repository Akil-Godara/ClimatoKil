# 🌍 ClimatoKil - South Asia Climate Data Archive

**ClimatoKil** is a comprehensive, open-access climate data visualization platform focused on South Asia. It tracks, analyzes, and visualizes long-term climate change impacts across **Afghanistan, Pakistan, and India** from 1960 to the present.

🔗 **Live Website:** [climatokil.pages.dev](https://climatokil.pages.dev) *(Update with your actual Cloudflare URL)*

---

##  About the Project

South Asia is one of the most climate-vulnerable regions in the world, home to nearly a quarter of the global population. ClimatoKil aims to make complex climate data accessible and understandable by providing interactive visualizations, geographic mapping, and structured data across 10 critical environmental indicators.

The platform serves as an educational resource for researchers, students, policymakers, and anyone interested in understanding the long-term environmental transformations in the region.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🇦🇫🇵🇰🇮🇳 **Country-Specific Analysis:** Deep dives into Afghanistan, Pakistan, and India.
-  **10 Climate Indicators:** Detailed data cards for Temperature, Precipitation, Wind Pressure, Atmospheric Gases, Cryosphere (Glaciers), Soil, Water Resources, El Niño/La Niña, Heatwaves, and Wildfires.
- 📈 **Interactive Data Visualization:** Dynamic charts built with Chart.js comparing historical trends across the three nations.
- ️ **Interactive Geographic Maps:** Leaflet.js-powered maps highlighting regional climate hotspots and country boundaries.
- 🏛️ **International Agency Directory:** Curated list of major space and climate agencies (IPCC, NOAA, NASA, ISRO, CNSA, ESA).
- 📚 **Open Data Resources:** Direct links to global climate databases and research portals (World Bank, UNFCCC, IMF, Climate Watch).

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

This is a lightweight, highly performant static website requiring no backend or build process.

- **Core:** HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Properties, Flexbox, Grid), Vanilla JavaScript
- **Data Visualization:** [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/)
- **Interactive Mapping:** [Leaflet.js](https://leafletjs.com/) & OpenStreetMap
- **Hosting & CDN:** [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/)
